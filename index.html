Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

Navigate to the "New Request" tab and fill out all required fields. Be sure to provide a detailed description and justification for your request. Once submitted, the request will be routed to the appropriate approver based on the amount and department.

An RFQ (Request for Quotation) is required for any purchase over R10,000 or when purchasing from a new vendor. The system will automatically prompt you to initiate an RFQ if these conditions are met.

You can view all your requests and their current status in the "My Requests" tab. The system will also notify you via email or Teams when there are updates to your request status.
